AroMR


2023
Yibo Wang, Ziqi Liu, Qi Lu

新兴的具备空间感知能力的混合现实 (MR) 平台让我们能够体验到数字对象与现实世界的融合，然而嗅觉在这种体验中的作用仍然很少被探索。为了解决这一问题，本文介绍了AroMR，这是一种通过紧凑、低成本的定制嗅觉设备和相应的MR平台将嗅觉反馈集成到MR中的创新方法。 Emerging Mixed Reality (MR) platforms with spatially aware capabilities allow us to experience digital objects blended with the real world, yet the role of the sense of smell in such experiences remains largely unexplored. To address this, this paper introduces AroMR, a novel integration of olfactory sensory feedback into MR through a compact, low-cost customized olfactory device and a corresponding MR platform.

简介 What is "Field-centric" Olfactory Simulation? 🤔

当前的全沉浸式VR气味显示器通常固定在头戴式显示器（HMD）上，或者设计成附在身体上的轻便设备。这些研究采用了类似的气味模拟策略，可以归纳为“以用户为中心”。然而，这样的设计通常提供有限的互动，无法模拟人类嗅觉感知的动态和空间特性。如果我们不是采用“以用户为中心”的策略，而是采用“以场景为中心”的方法，将气味显示器（OD）从用户的鼻子移动到整个环境中呢？这将把嗅觉界面从可穿戴设备转变为环境系统。 Current olfactory displays for fully immersive VR are typically affixed to head-mounted displays (HMDs) or designed as lightweight devices attached to the body. These studies represent a similar olfactory simulation strategy that can be concluded as "user-centric." However, such designs often offer limited interaction and fail to simulate the dynamic and spatial nature of human olfactory perception. What if, instead of a "user-centric" strategy, we could adopt a "field-centric" approach by relocating olfactory displays (ODs) from the user’s nose to the entire ambient environment? This would transform the olfactory interface from a wearable device to an environmental system.

硬件设计与开发 Hardware Design and Development 🛠

为了实现气味播放装置的自动控制，本系统采用ESP32微控制器作为控制板，并使用ArduinoIDE作为代码开发环境进行系统开发。在整体组装结构设计方面，该装置主要分为三个模块，分别是气味播放模块、气味储存模块和PCB模块。 In order to realize the automatic control of the odor playback device, this system uses ESP32 microcontroller as the control board and ArduinoIDE as the code development environment to develop the system technologically. In terms of the overall assembly structure design, the device is mainly divided into three modules, which are the odor playback module, the odor storage module, and the PCB module.

软件应用设计与开发 Put it in MR Environment! 🏞

为了帮助混合现实平台实现空间感知和体现式互动，我们选择了具有相应技术条件的硬件平台 - 由Meta提供的Oculus Quest 3作为软件载体，Unity引擎作为软件开发平台，以及Meta XR SDK作为开发工具 - 来开发一个基于混合现实的沉浸式平台。 In order to help the mixed reality platform realize spatial perception and embodied interaction, we choose a hardware platform with corresponding technical conditions - Oculus Quest 3 provided by Meta as the software carrier, Unity engine as the software development platform, and Meta XR SDK as the development tool - to develop a mixed reality-based immersive reality platform

后续工作 Future Work? 🤔

目前我们进行了小范围的内部实验，计划后续寻找被试进行用户实验。我很希望听到他们对于“Field-centric”这种气味渲染策略的看法和接受程度。

另外，更令我感兴趣的是，他们可能会用什么样的方法来摆放和使用这样的播放器？对应什么样的虚拟景观？如何利用房间环境？这些是很有趣的问题，或许可以引出对于设计空间的探讨。 Currently, we have conducted small-scale internal experiments and plan to recruit participants for user testing in the future. I am very eager to hear their opinions of the "Field-centric" olfactory rendering strategy.

Moreover, I am particularly interested in how they might place and use such a player, what kind of virtual landscapes they would pair it with, and how they would utilize the room environment. These are fascinating questions that could potentially lead to discussions about the design space.



TagMagnet


2023
合作项目：刘子琦，刘佳成
 指导老师：关琰 2023
Teamwork: Ziqi Liu, Jiacheng Liu
Advisor: Yan Guan

选择将这个项目放上来的原因是，虽然它只是个概念设计与交互原型，很多技术问题我们并没有想清楚，但它引出了许多有趣的讨论，也带引发我许多反思与思考。 The reason for showcasing this project is that, although it is just a conceptual design and interaction prototype, and we haven't fully resolved many technical issues, it has sparked numerous interesting discussions and prompted a lot of reflection and thought.

项目构思 Why do we need Tagmagnet? 🤔

我们将目标用户设置为那些喜欢随手记录生活，但并没有过多时间去整理的用户。他们有着对生活的仪式感和怀旧的倾向，但却由于照片的杂乱很少翻看手机相册（可能就是我俩的写照）。 Our target users are those who enjoy capturing moments of their daily lives but don't have much time to organize their photos. They have a sense of ceremony and a tendency towards nostalgia but seldom browse their photo albums due to the clutter (which might just be a reflection of the two of us).

解决方案 How to address this? 🤔

我们希望通过一些交互和信息结构、算法上的设计来解决以上的问题。我们的基本是通过图片识别与手动添加结合的方式为图片添加tag，之后基于这些tag来进行自动归类与相册推荐。 We aim to address these issues through interaction design, information structure, and algorithmic solutions. Our basic approach is to add tags to images using a combination of image recognition and manual input. Based on these tags, we can then automatically categorize images and recommend albums.

Tag添加流程设计 How to Add Tags? 😥

添加tag的方式分为自动、半自动和手动三种。 Tagging methods are divided into three categories: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual.

  • 自动添加的信息包括时间地点等基本信息与图片类型（风景、人物）和图片内容（人物、摆设、场景）等目前图像识别能够分析的内容 Automatic Tagging: This includes basic information such as time and location, as well as image type (landscape, portrait) and content (people, objects, scenes) that current image recognition can analyze.
  • 半自动添加指用户设置某些添加规则，如中午拍摄的带有食物内容的图片自动打上“午餐”tag Semi-Automatic Tagging: Users set certain tagging rules, such as automatically tagging images taken at noon with food content as "lunch."
  • 手动添加则是用户在拍摄是相机界面为之后一段时间拍摄的图片加上某一tag或拍摄完成后添加 Manual Tagging: Users can add tags through the camera interface for photos taken within a certain period or add tags after the photo is taken.

影集建立方式 Draw out Tags like a Magnet! 🧲

在有了带tag的照片后，用户可以根据tag快速地建立一个影集。在创建影集是用户可以选择影集所选择的tag，比如一个影集如果有“抽象表情”“室友”“多人”三个tag，那么该影集就会自动过滤选择出照片中同时带有这三个tag的图片进入影集；同时，如果之后也有图片被打上了这三个标签，那么也会被该影集收录。 Once photos are tagged, users can quickly create an album based on these tags. When creating an album, users can select the desired tags for the album. For example, an album with the tags "abstract expressions," "roommates," and "group" will automatically filter and include photos that have all three tags. Additionally, any future photos tagged with these three tags will also be included in the album.

回忆推荐页面 Find the Lost Memories 💾

除此之外，在相册的回忆页面也会为用户推送一些回忆影集，如上周你最亲近的人，去年今天你的相片等。这些影集同样是基于tag筛选生成，可以被用户修改和保存。 Additionally, the memories page of the album will suggest some memory albums to users, such as "your closest people from last week" or "your photos from today last year." These albums are also generated based on tag filtering and can be modified and saved by the user.

交互原型 Here's the Prototype! 📸

作为一个经常批量处理照片的摄影爱好者，影集这种归类方式也是收到lightroom的启发。导入Lightroom的图片本质上是建立了一系列索引，用户可以任意归类却不改变源文件的存储。同样的，在tagmagnet中用户依然可以按时间顺序查找图片，但当他们想归类和回忆时，他们可以很轻易的找出他们想要的图片。 As a photography enthusiast who frequently processes photos in bulk, the album categorization approach in TagMagnet was inspired by Lightroom. In Lightroom, importing photos essentially creates a series of indexes, allowing users to categorize images without altering the original files. Similarly, in TagMagnet, users can still search for photos chronologically, but when it comes to categorization and reminiscing, they can easily locate the images they want.

总的来说相册内的信息可以被用来做很多有趣的事情，重建早已忘记的回忆、以一些奇怪的方式重新认识自己等等。但最重要的问题是，相册内的个人信息比任何软件都来得敏感。或许等到本地运行的大模型真正全方位落地到手机这样算力的设备上后，相册能够真正变得有趣且安全。 Overall, the information within photo albums can be used for many interesting purposes, such as reconstructing forgotten memories or rediscovering oneself in unusual ways. However, the most critical issue is that personal information in photo albums is more sensitive than in any other software. Perhaps once large models that run locally on devices like smartphones are fully realized, photo albums can truly become both engaging and secure.



AI-Assisted Art Training


2023
Chen Liang, Chang Liu, Ziqi Liu, Yao Lu, Xuezhu Wang, Tianyu Lin, Yue Wen, Siyuan Feng (The order of authors has not yet been determined.)

我对AI生成内容的方式一直有些疑虑。AI使用完整的图像作为训练材料，忽略了创作过程中的关键细节，如图层、笔触、速度、力度和姿态等。这种像素级的学习与人类的创作过程本质上不同。当单纯使用AI从提示生成结果时，这种差异可能不明显，但随着人机协作的深入，AI对创作过程的理解缺失将成为关键问题。因此，AI在许多行业中难以融入传统工作流程，这也是AI迄今未对就业产生大规模替代效应的原因之一。 I have always had some concerns about how AI generates content. AI uses complete images as training material, missing critical creation process details like layers, brush strokes, speed, pressure, and posture. This pixel-level learning is fundamentally different from human creativity. While this discrepancy may be subtle when generating results from prompts, it becomes a critical issue as human-AI collaboration deepens. Thus, AI struggles to integrate into traditional workflows, contributing to its limited impact on employment replacement.

项目构思 Where Should We Start? 🤔

让AI理解创作过程的很重要的一点是，哪些信息与数据是重要的。例如在国画创作中墨的干湿非常重要，但在素描中运笔速度和压力会是重点。我们最终选择先从影响因素较为简单、单幅作品数据量较少的书法入手。通过和一些书法练习者和教学者的讨论我们决定将压力、运笔速度和笔身倾角作为重点关注的数据。 A crucial aspect of helping AI understand the creative process is identifying which information and data are important. For example, in traditional Chinese painting, the wetness or dryness of the ink is critical, whereas in sketching, the speed and pressure of the strokes are more significant. We have ultimately decided to start with calligraphy, where the influencing factors are relatively simple, and the data volume per piece is smaller. After discussions with calligraphy practitioners and educators, we have decided to focus on pressure, stroke speed, and brush angle as key data points.

数据采集应用开发 Develop Penbrush and App! 👨🏻‍💻

虽然目前Ipad上有一些适合书法练习的应用，如Procreate等，但一方面，这些应用使用时的内部数据我们无法获取，同时其笔刷引擎相关的参数和算法也没有开源；另一方面，我们经过讨论选择了楷书和隶书的两个经典字帖《多宝塔碑》和《礼器碑》作为被试临摹对象，但从我们的初步访谈和讨论的结果来看《多宝塔碑》的很多较为刚硬的笔锋很难用目前可以找到的笔刷写出，这会对书写者的手感有较大影响。综合考虑之后我们决定开发一个简单的临帖APP和适用于这两个字帖的笔刷。 Although some iPad apps like Procreate are suitable for calligraphy, they don't provide access to their internal data or brush engine details. We selected two classic calligraphy examples, "Duo Bao Ta Bei" and "Li Qi Bei," but found that existing brushes struggle with the rigid strokes of "Duo Bao Ta Bei," affecting the writing experience. Therefore, we decided to develop a simple tracing app with custom brushes for these calligraphy examples.

数据采集与用户访谈 Collect Data! ✍🏻

在数据采集流程的设计上，我们包含了四部分的书写任务：多宝塔碑和礼器碑的原帖临摹,和给出碑文的黑体字版本让用户在风格上有自由发挥空间的书写。我们希望在有限的时间里采集到尽可能多样但有代表性的数据 In designing the data collection process, we included four writing tasks: tracing the original "Duo Bao Ta Bei" and "Li Qi Bei" calligraphy, and writing given text in a sans-serif style with room for stylistic freedom. Our goal is to gather as diverse and representative data as possible within a limited timeframe.

从目前在团队内部展开的测试来看主要有以下的反馈：笔刷的效果确实比市面上大部分笔刷更加适用于多宝塔碑的笔锋摹写，但在界面安排上写字的框格过大，不在手腕能够控制的范围内；笔顺对于后续训练的影响会很大，但即使熟练的书法练习者也会对某些字的笔画顺序拿不准；原定的200字的书写任务量过大，一个小时很难完成，需要进行精简。我们对此做出针对性的优化后投入了正式的数据采集。 Based on the current internal team tests, the feedback mainly includes the following points: The brush effect is indeed more suitable for emulating the brushstrokes of the Duobao Pagoda Stele compared to most brushes on the market. However, the writing frame on the interface is too large, falling outside the range that can be controlled by the wrist. The stroke order significantly impacts subsequent training, yet even experienced calligraphy practitioners may be unsure of the stroke order for certain characters. The original task of writing 200 characters is excessive and difficult to complete within an hour, necessitating a reduction in the number of characters. After making targeted optimizations, we proceeded with formal data collection.


算法设计与模型训练 How to Make Large Model Understand? 🤖

与传统训练使用的书法作品图片不同，我们采集到的数据本质上讲还是序列信息。我们预期采用的方法是通过自回归的方式，mask掉一部分的数据，通过重新生成与原信息比对的方式进行训练。这会包含两个部分： Unlike traditional training that uses images of calligraphy works, the data we collect is essentially sequential information. Our expected approach involves using an autoregressive method, masking a portion of the data, and then regenerating it for comparison with the original information. This process consists of two parts:

  • 笔画内部（一个stroke内的数据），mask掉某一段数据重新生成，这可能会有益于ai理解笔画的走向、力度、运笔速度等信息 Within a stroke (data within a single stroke), a segment of data is masked and regenerated, which might help the AI understand stroke direction, pressure, and speed.
  • mask掉一个字中的某一笔画，对此去进行重新生成，这可能有益于ai理解间架结构和笔画关系 Masking an entire stroke within a character and regenerating it, which could aid the AI in understanding the structure and relationships between strokes.

坦白讲我目前对于训练的效果并没有十足的把握，因为可供训练的数据还是太少。目前数据采集正在进行中，后续的进度我会实时更新！ To be honest, I am not entirely confident about the training outcomes, as the available data is still quite limited. Data collection is currently ongoing, and I will provide real-time updates on our progress!

这个设计是大二刚刚接触设计的时候所做的。那时我对设计的认识还停留在“画图”和“好看”的标准，但这一个项目真正让我感受到设计要考虑的远不止此。如何让新的设计与原有的环境统一，如何从用户的角度找到合理的设计思路，这些远比自我陶醉的美观更加重要。 This design project was undertaken during my sophomore year when I had just started learning about design. At that time, my understanding of design was limited to "drawing" and "aesthetics." However, this project made me realize that design considerations extend far beyond that. Integrating new designs with existing environments and finding reasonable design approaches from the user's perspective are far more important than mere visual appeal.

前期调研 Take a Look Around the Dormitory 👀

前期我们考察了目标楼的整体结构、内部环境和其现有的导视系统，以希望我们的设计能够最大程度地契合楼内结构和环境风格，并且解决现有导视的一些问题。毫无疑问我们还是发现了诸多问题，这次的考察也为我们提供了一些思路，我们在之后的设计中会有所回应。 Initially, we examined the overall structure, internal environment, and existing wayfinding system of the target building. Our goal was to ensure that our design would seamlessly integrate with the building's structure and environmental style while addressing some of the issues with the current wayfinding system. Unsurprisingly, we identified numerous problems. This assessment also provided us with some ideas that we will address in our subsequent design.

系统设计 Design of the System 🤔

首先我们对导视的重点作了界定：我们认为在宿舍楼中，对于导视的需求主要集中在大厅的公共空间，这里房间的功能多样，同时有较多外来人员流动；而住宿区域一方面房间的类型较为单一，另一方面楼内的“原住民”对于导视几乎没有需求，故这一部分不是重点。 Firstly, we defined the key areas for the wayfinding system: we believe that in the dormitory building, the primary need for wayfinding is concentrated in the public spaces of the lobby. This area has diverse room functions and a higher flow of external visitors. In contrast, the residential areas have more uniform room types, and the "residents" of the building have little need for wayfinding. Therefore, this part is not our focus.

图标设计 Icon Design ✏️

考虑到整体装修环境的配色和导视图标醒目的原则，我们采用黑白作为图标的整体配色；在图标的形态结构方面，单线条的设计在整体较为稳重的环境中显得过于跳脱，而色块形式的结构与文字相配合时又显得重量失衡，所以我们选择了一个相对折中的风格——多线条重叠的方式。 Considering the color scheme of the overall decor and the principle of making wayfinding icons noticeable, we chose black and white as the primary colors for the icons. In terms of icon design, single-line designs appeared too conspicuous in the relatively subdued environment, while blocky designs seemed unbalanced when combined with text. Therefore, we opted for a more balanced style—using overlapping lines.

导视板设计 Design of Wayfinding Signage 💡

25cm的黑色木板，出于削减黑白图标板带来的古板单调之感的目的，同时考虑到新雅楼的整体配色，我们决定用原木色木板作为图标板的一个承托，以向左向上各错位5cm的方式放置在其后。针对不同的位置和用途，我们设计了三种不同的导视板。 For the 25 cm black wooden boards, to reduce the dullness of black-and-white icons and considering the overall color scheme of the New Yalou Building, we decided to use natural wood-colored boards as a backdrop for the icons. These boards are placed offset by 5 cm to the left and upwards. We designed three different types of wayfinding boards to accommodate various locations and purposes.
